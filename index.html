About

Director, producer and creative technologist weaving digital art, XR technologies and social justice. Founder of CANVAR and co-founder of MAIX and XRCol, I lead immersive projects that spotlight underrepresented narratives. As a Sundance Institute: Art of Practice fellow and member of Unity for Humanity and XR LATAM, I guide artists and communities in creating interactive, memory-driven and socially engaged experiences.
